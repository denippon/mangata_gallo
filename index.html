Exceptional Beauty for Unforgettable Moments

Creating special pieces for special moments.

Diamonds are forever.

Mangata and Gallo is a jewelry store that specializes in special occasions like engagements, weddings and anniversaries.

Mangata & Gallo's selection of jewelry is known for its high-quality and classic look.

From the comfort of your home or eye to eye, the choice is yours.

The jewelry company primarily operates online and has a small storefront in Austin, Texas with an atelier attached for browsing.

Meet the creator

Mariana is the owner and the lead designer of Mangata and Gallo.

After graduating from design school with a specialization in diamond cutting and metal smithing, Mariana opened a store in her hometown, Austin and started to grow her business online.
